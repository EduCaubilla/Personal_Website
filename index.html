About

Let me tell you about myself

I'm a Xamarin Developer and Full Stack Developer with almost two years working with Xamarin Forms and Native, another two years with the javascript environment and about 4 more years collaborating on development projects with CMS and other no code platforms like Weebly.

After more than 10 years as a freelance Video Producer working with many brands and agencies in a lot of projects. In 2016 I started to get involved in web development, at first collaborating with copies and design but soon started to find programming very rewarding so I joined a bootcamp to study proper programming and developed several websites with different technologies from vanilla Javascript with no database to MEAN stack with MongoDB.

Almost three years ago, in fall 2020, I landed my first job in a company, EricSports, developing a Javascript based online tool with ThreeJs called EricBoard a 2D/3D animated digital whiteboard for different sports addressed to technical staff. The tool was almost finished but not yet on production so I had to rewrite some features to make them more intuitive, add some new functionalities, fix bugs and refactor and achieving a successful launch as the tool was adopted by several hundreds users in a short period. Once EricBoard was on production and working smoothly I was repositioned to another project, EricVideo (from the suite EricOne), a video analysis cross platform software for sports professionals that was in the final stages of development. I had to learn .NET | C# and Xamarin Forms really fast and start to collaborate in different tasks with the team including building and deploying on all four platforms. Almost a year later was the final launch with a discreet welcome at first, but soon the user's bulk increased quite much, and once the software was stable and getting grip in the market I was put in charge of the app with the mission of improve and add some new features to launch a second version with I did quite successfully. At this point I had the feeling that my cycle came to and end after two years and a half and decided to change and before move to another role I provided all the assistance needed to make the transition as smooth as possible.

In spring 2023 I started working for Capitole Consulting for a client related to airlines in their in-house mobile app addressed to their own cabin crew and based on a task calendar built in Xamarin Native. I learnt a lot of design in native iOS with Xcode and Android with AndroidStudio. Unfortunately after almost four months, the company considers I have not passed the trial period so I was let go.

At the moment, in summer 2023 I'm updating some side projects that were a bit disregarded so I can refresh my Javascript knowledge and also planning to learn more about native mobile development, probably focusing on iOS with Swift and SwiftUI.

I enjoy very much learning new things to apply to projects and deliver my best. Those who know me say I'm honest, focused and perseverant but also on the down side I can be too much of a perfectionist, which leads me to be slow in some cases and for some I can be very straightforward at times as well.
